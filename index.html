STORE POLICIES

Shipping Policy

  • Nationwide shipping via LBC (insured with tracking)
  • Metro Manila: 3-5 business days
  • Provincial areas: 5-7 business days
  • All packages are sealed, insured, and tracked

Payment Options

  • Cash on Delivery (COD) available nationwide
  • GCash, BDO, BPI, PayMaya accepted
